Reflections & Conclusion

Research Method

The main research method is contextual inquiry. The activities we intended to observe are:

  1. Where does a user go on a daily basis?

  2. With whom does the user interact on a daily basis outside of his/her family?

  3. How often does a user utilize mobile applications for translations, interactive games?

Main Goals

  • Facilitate simple, quick, on-the-spot translations for users through daily routines

  • Enhance usersâ€™ vocabulary acquisition through Word Generator and Dictionary features

  • Provide users interactive games, such as a Crossword Puzzle feature, to practice vocabulary and sentence comprehension

Conclusion

Creating Translate Mate was a rewarding and insightful project that combined technology and language to fix a lingering issue for immigrants and refugees who struggle with language barriers. Through the development process, we gained a deeper understanding of user interface design and the importance of accessibility in app development. The app not only provides real-time translations across multiple languages but also aims to foster cross-cultural communication and understanding. The app also includes 4 different methods of learning, which helped us visualize what can be done to fix this issue. This project highlighted how technology can be a powerful tool in making the world more connected
