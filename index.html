TRANSLATE MATE

Designing for Diversity

Project Overview

We developed a Translator App, a mobile application whose target audience is the immigrant and refugee community as part of the theme, “Designing for Diversity.” There are many daily challenges this community faces that many take for granted due to a language barrier. There are varying levels of proficiency in English, but all in the community still face similar daily challenges because English is not their first language. English proficiency can range from a graduate student, who is fluent in English and their own language, to one of our grandmothers who does not speak English at all. The app can enhance any user’s vocabulary, but we are specifically looking through the lens of the immigrant and refugee community in our design development.

Our primary Translator feature will help with daily routines, but additional features encourage users to learn new vocabulary and to practice through interactive games. A Word Generator feature will encourage users to enhance their vocabulary by swiping through a randomly generated list. Users can also search for words in the Dictionary feature. After learning more words, eventually users will be able to progress to sentences and apply sentences to crossword puzzle clues.

Key Features

  • Facilitate simple, quick, on-the-spot translations for users through daily routines

  • Enhance users’ vocabulary acquisition through Word Generator and Dictionary features

  • Provide users interactive games, such as a Crossword Puzzle feature, to practice vocabulary and sentence comprehension
